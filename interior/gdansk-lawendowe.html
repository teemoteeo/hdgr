Gdańsk Lawendowe Wzgórze

2024

Year

2024

Type

Interior Design

Location

Poland

An interior inspired by mid-century modern aesthetics, where warmth meets a touch of character. A palette of brick red, burgundy, and beige builds a cosy, layered atmosphere that envelops the entire space.

Decorative mouldings on the walls add edge and an elegant rhythm, while modern accents — an organic chrome mirror, minimalist wall lights — introduce a fresh, contemporary twist. Heavy dark curtains and materials of contrasting textures complete the mood, making the space not only stylish but genuinely pleasant to live in.
