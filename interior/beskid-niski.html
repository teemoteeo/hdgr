Beskid Niski

2024

Year

2024

Type

Interior Design

Location

Poland

The interior design of this holiday cottage in the Beskid Niski mountains is rooted in the idea of merging contemporary form with the local mountain spirit. The priority was to create a space that feels modern yet close to nature and the atmosphere of the region.

Dominant wood warms the interiors and eases the mind, forming the backbone of the entire composition. Pastel accents in tiles and decorative paints complement the natural base, lending the space lightness and subtle energy. Colour serves both a decorative and symbolic role: bedrooms in yellow and pink create comfort and safety; the bathroom and hallway use green for calm; the kitchen is dominated by blue for cleanliness; the living room holds a neutral palette for quiet rest.

The second floor is an open, multifunctional area combining a recreation zone with a play space for children — a coherent, contemporary interior deeply rooted in the natural character of the Beskid Niski.
