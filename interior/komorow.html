Year

2024

Type

Interior Design

The kitchen design is built around an ergonomic functional layout and high-quality materials that ensure durability and a timeless character. The island serves as the central workspace, providing a comfortable area for meal preparation and naturally integrating the kitchen with the dining zone.

The introduction of warm-toned wood and cohesive mineral surfaces brings harmony to the interior, while carefully selected task and decorative lighting emphasizes the texture of the materials used. The result is a kitchen that unites everyday comfort, aesthetic balance, and the durability essential for daily life.
